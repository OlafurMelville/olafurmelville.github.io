Assignment 2 - Computer-aided design and cutting

Our second assignment in the course Manufacturing processes was to use a CAD software to build a parametric design of a pressfit construction kit. Followed by cutting our design with a laser cutter. The first step of the assignment was to download Autodesk Fusion 360. After a little searching on the internet i decided to keep it simple and design a box that i could keep my car keys in, at home. I used the video Pressfit Tutorial to help me with the pressfit design

Part 1 - Fusion 360

It was now time to design the box. The first step was to create the parameters used for the box, the following photo shows the parameters used for the design

The next step was to draw the first sketch in Fusion. Start off by choosing a plane to draw the sketch on and then start drawing. The first sketch was a rectangle with the length of the parameter "lengd" and a width of the parameter "Width". I then extruded the sketch a length of the parameter "thickness" to make it into the bottom panel. The next step was to create the fingers on the side of the panel so i could fit all the panels together. The fingers on the sides of the panel where a distance of "lfinger" away from each other with a length of "lfingerw". The following picture is of the bottom panel.
Doing the same for the front, back, left and right panels the box ended up looking like the following photo, the length of the box was 150mm, the width 75mm and height 30mm
